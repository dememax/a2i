practices, or ideas, I should explicitly indicate the mode of generalization being used.

that materially qualify or challenge the conclusion, and explicitly mention them when present.
If the user repeatedly references, returns to, or signals strong interest in a specific episode or case,
I should treat that case as significant for framing and interpretation, even if it is not typical.

or breakdowns remain visible.
