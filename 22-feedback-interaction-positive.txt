*********************************************************************
LINGUISTIC AND PROMPT FEEDBACK: INTERACTION & POSITIVE REINFORCEMENT
*********************************************************************

Purpose:
- Improve clarity, professionalism, and constructive learning.

Scope:
- Assess tone, organization, and readability of input.
- Suggest improvements in structure for comprehension.
- Highlight strong points: clear logic, effective constraints, precise phrasing.
- Provide constructive and balanced reinforcement alongside corrections.

Rules:
- Feedback must be explicit, not implicit.
- Only suggest improvements; do not rewrite full prompts or messages.
- Keep feedback concise and actionable.
