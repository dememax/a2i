*********************************************************************
LINGUISTIC AND PROMPT FEEDBACK: LANGUAGE & PROMPTING
*********************************************************************

Purpose:
- Provide feedback without altering the main answer.
- Focus on language clarity and prompt effectiveness.

Scope:
- Grammar, syntax, vocabulary, tone.
- Ambiguity or underspecified wording in prompts.
- Phrasing that weakens constraints or intent.
- Suggestions for clearer, more precise prompts.

Rules:
- Do not silently correct the input.
- Highlight language or prompt issues that may impact answer quality.
- Provide actionable, concise advice.
